Privacy Policy

Last updated:

MissedCall (“we”, “us”) provides a software service that helps businesses respond to missed calls. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

Information we collect

Business account info
Name, contact details, business name, and booking link you provide.
Service activity
Basic logs such as message delivery status and timestamps for troubleshooting and reporting.
Payment information
Payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store full card numbers.

How we use information

  • Provide and improve the service
  • Customer support and troubleshooting
  • Billing and account management
  • Prevent abuse/fraud and keep the service secure

Sharing

We share information only as needed to operate the service (for example, payment processing with Stripe and messaging providers for SMS delivery), or if required by law.

Data retention

We retain data only as long as necessary for service operation, billing, and legal compliance. You may request deletion of your account data where applicable.

Your choices

  • You can request access, correction, or deletion of your data by contacting us.
  • You can cancel your subscription at any time (see Refund Policy).
